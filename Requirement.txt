
Here are the steps you can follow to install Spyder and Jupyter on your machine:

1. Install Anaconda: Spyder and Jupyter are both included in the Anaconda distribution of Python. To install Anaconda, you can download the installer from the official website (https://www.anaconda.com/products/distribution/) and run it on your machine.

2. Open Anaconda Navigator: After installing Anaconda, you can open Anaconda Navigator from the start menu or by typing anaconda-navigator in the command prompt.

3. Launch Spyder: Once Anaconda Navigator is open, you can launch Spyder by clicking on the "Launch" button under the Spyder icon.

4. Launch Jupyter Notebook: You can launch Jupyter Notebook by clicking on the "Launch" button under the Jupyter Notebook icon.

5. Open Jupyter Notebook from command line: You can also open Jupyter notebook by running the command jupyter-notebook in the command prompt or terminal.

6. Open Spyder from command line: You can also open Spyder by running the command spyder in the command prompt or terminal.

Please keep in mind that these steps may vary slightly depending on your operating system.
